🍝regatonni-alavadka

Description

This is a delicous pasta called regatonni-alavadka made at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb rigatoni pasta
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 small yellow onion or 2 shallots, minced
  • 3–4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup tomato paste
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (or more, to taste)
  • ½ cup vodka
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan or Pecorino Romano cheese, plus more for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
